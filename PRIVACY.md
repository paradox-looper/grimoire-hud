# Privacy Policy — Celestia

**Last Updated:** April 17, 2026

## Summary

Celestia respects your privacy. **No personal data leaves your device.** All astrological calculations run locally in your browser.

## What Data Celestia Handles

### Data You Enter
- Birth name, date, time, and location
- Optional notes or profile labels

### Where It's Stored
- **Only on your device**, in your browser's local storage
- Never transmitted to any server
- Never sold, shared, or analyzed

### What Celestia Does NOT Collect
- No analytics
- No tracking cookies
- No user accounts
- No passwords
- No credit card information
- No advertising IDs

## Third-Party Services

Celestia uses the following third-party services, each with a specific limited purpose:

1. **OpenStreetMap Nominatim** — When you search for a city, your search query (e.g. "Paris, France") is sent to `nominatim.openstreetmap.org` to retrieve geographic coordinates. No personal data accompanies the request. See [OSM Privacy Policy](https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy).

2. **Google Fonts** — Typefaces are loaded from `fonts.googleapis.com` on first visit, then cached. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

3. **unpkg / jsdelivr** — React library is loaded from a CDN on first visit, then cached. These are static content CDNs and do not track users beyond standard web server logs.

## Your Rights

Since no data is collected, there is nothing for us to delete or modify on your behalf. To clear your local data, you can:
- Use the "Clear All Profiles" button in Settings
- Clear your browser's local storage for the site

## Offline Mode

After the first visit, Celestia caches all necessary files and works fully offline with no internet connection required.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/paradox-looper/celestia).

## Children's Privacy

Celestia does not collect any information from anyone, including users under 13. Parents should supervise minors' use of astrology applications.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the "Last Updated" date at the top will change. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
